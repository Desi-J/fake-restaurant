About GrubPub

History

Originally starting out as a three person garage band. The audience complained that the music was awful and there was no food. Attempting to please the people they started cooking. Everyone enjoyed the food and even came back. Not long later Grub Pub was formed out of a hole in the wall. (literally)

Family Owned

This business is family owned so naturally it is family friendly. Feel free to bring your kids, parents, and even your grandparents. We've got meals for the kids and drinks for the adults. (We don't sell alcohol to minors.)

Staff

This restaurant is a revolving door... of opportunity. People from all around work here to gain experience and enrich their lives with pub culture.
